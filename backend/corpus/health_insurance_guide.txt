
            Health Insurance Overview:
            Health insurance covers medical expenses and healthcare costs.
            Types include HMO, PPO, EPO, and High-Deductible Health Plans.

            Key components:
            - Premium: Monthly payment for coverage
            - Deductible: Amount paid before insurance kicks in
            - Copayment: Fixed amount for doctor visits
            - Coinsurance: Percentage of costs after deductible
            - Out-of-pocket maximum: Yearly limit on your expenses

            Essential benefits covered:
            - Ambulatory patient services
            - Emergency services
            - Hospitalization
            - Maternity and newborn care
            - Mental health and substance abuse
            - Prescription drugs
            - Rehabilitative services
            - Laboratory services
            - Preventive care
            - Pediatric services
