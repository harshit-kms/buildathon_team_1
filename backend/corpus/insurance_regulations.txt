
            INSURANCE REGULATIONS OVERVIEW

            Federal Regulations:
            - McCarran-Ferguson Act: Delegates insurance regulation to states
            - HIPAA: Health insurance portability and privacy
            - ACA (Affordable Care Act): Health insurance requirements
            - ERISA: Employee benefits regulation
            - Fair Credit Reporting Act: Use of credit in underwriting

            State Regulations:
            - Licensing requirements for agents and companies
            - Policy form approvals
            - Rate regulations and filings
            - Claims handling standards
            - Consumer protection laws
            - Market conduct examinations

            Key Compliance Areas:
            - Anti-fraud measures and reporting
            - Privacy and data protection
            - Fair dealing and disclosure requirements
            - Suitability standards for sales
            - Continuing education requirements
            - Record keeping and documentation
