
            Life Insurance Basics:
            Life insurance provides financial protection for your family after your death. 
            Term life insurance is temporary coverage for specific periods (10, 20, 30 years).
            Whole life insurance provides permanent coverage with cash value component.
            Universal life insurance offers flexible premiums and death benefits.

            Key benefits of life insurance:
            - Income replacement for dependents
            - Debt coverage including mortgages
            - Final expense coverage
            - Estate planning tool
            - Business protection for key employees

            Factors affecting premiums:
            - Age and health status
            - Lifestyle habits (smoking, drinking)
            - Occupation and hobbies
            - Coverage amount and type
            - Medical exam results
